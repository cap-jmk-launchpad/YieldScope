# Phase 1 scope honesty: Binance, OKX, Monad, LUNC — and nothing else yet

## The locked pitch

**Solving scattered rewards. Track all your crypto rewards in one place.**

“All” in the pitch means *all of the streams we actually sync* — not every protocol on every chain. Phase 1 is depth over breadth.

## What ships now

| Source | What we sync |
|--------|----------------|
| Binance | Simple Earn rewards / interest history |
| OKX | Savings / Simple Earn interest streams |
| Monad | Staking unclaimed + accrued rewards |
| LUNC | Claimed + pending Terra Classic stake rewards |

## Explicitly out of scope (for now)

ETH / Lido / Base / multi-chain DeFi aggregators, tax engines, APY farm browsers, full portfolio valuation. Those are Phase 2+ only after Phase 1 DoD is green.

## How voice stays honest

Marketing may say “rewards” and “one place.” Product copy and docs name the four sources. Differentiator remains **earn-only ledger + Monad checkpoint** — not “we track everything.”

## Why this is a feature

Scattered rewards get worse when tools pretend coverage they do not have. Fail-closed chips (`ok | error | not_connected`) beat a green zero from an invented adapter.
