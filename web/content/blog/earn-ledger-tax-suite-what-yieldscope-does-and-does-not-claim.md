# Earn ledger ≠ tax suite: what YieldScope does and does not claim

## Different job

Tax software exists to classify disposals, cost basis, and jurisdiction rules. Portfolio trackers exist to value bags. YieldScope exists to answer: **what did I earn across these venues?**

## What we claim

- Earn-only sync for Phase 1 sources.
- One ledger with honest source status.
- Optional Monad checkpoint for a sync window.

## What we do not claim

- Tax exports or filing advice.
- Full DeFi coverage (Zerion’s job).
- APY farm discovery.

If your primary need is taxes, use a tax product. If your primary pain is scattered rewards, use a rewards ledger.
