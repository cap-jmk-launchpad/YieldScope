# Adding LUNC stake rewards to a multi-venue earn ledger

## Why LUNC is in Phase 1

LUNC pending staking rewards via public LCD are another “I earned something somewhere else” stream. Ignoring them recreates the spreadsheet.

YieldScope accepts a Terra Classic `terra1…` address (or explorer link) and syncs pending stake rewards as current rows — same fail-closed rules as other sources.

## Honest limits

- Pending rewards are a snapshot, not a full historical earn series.
- We are not a Terra portfolio explorer.
- More chains come only after Phase 1 DoD is green.

Scattered rewards stay scattered until every stream that matters to you has a row — with a clear source label.
