# Rewards ledger vs DeFi portfolio: different jobs, different tools

## Portfolio apps are great — at portfolios

Zerion, DeBank, and peers shine at onchain positions. They are weak at first-class CEX Simple Earn history and Monad-native attestation of *your* earn window.

## Earn-native whitespace

YieldScope bridges CeFi earn + Monad stake for the personal “what did I make?” question — with depth on a few sources instead of shallow coverage of everything.

## Use both if you need both

Nothing stops you from keeping a DeFi portfolio app for positions and YieldScope for rewards. Different surfaces, different truth.

Solving scattered rewards is not the same as mapping every DeFi position.
