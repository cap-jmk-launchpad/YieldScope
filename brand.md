# YieldScope brand

## Essence

YieldScope is the **personal earn ledger** — calm, precise, proof-oriented. Not a casino APY shop. Not a tax suite. A place where “what did I earn?” has one trustworthy answer.

## Voice

- Direct, slightly technical, never hype.
- Prefer “earned” over “yield farming.” Prefer “checkpoint” over “NFT proof.”
- No emoji in product chrome. No rocket-ship marketing.

## Name treatment

**YieldScope** — one word, capital Y and S. Never “Yield Scope,” never “YS” in hero copy.

On brand surfaces the wordmark is the hero signal. Headlines support it; they do not outrank it.

## Audience

Builders and power users who already earn on Binance / OKX and stake on Monad, and who care that a number can be verified — not just screenshotted.

## Promise

Sync the three streams that matter for Phase 1. Show only real data. Attest a root on Monad.

## Anti-promise

We do not claim full DeFi coverage, tax exports, or farm discovery in Phase 1.
