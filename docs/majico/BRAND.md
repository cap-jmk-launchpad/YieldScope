## 1. Brand identity

- Name: YieldScope
- Tagline/one-liner: Solving scattered rewards. Track all your crypto rewards in one place.
- Category/niche: standout earnings checkpoint for CEX earn programs and staking networks (Binance Simple Earn, OKX savings, Monad staking rewards) within the passive income crypto sector

### Mission
We help Builders and power users who earn liquidity incentives on major exchanges like Binance and OKX while simultaneously staking assets such as Monad and LUNC across decentralized protocols. Our mission is to provide one trustworthy answer regarding their actual earnings rather than offering a complex portfolio dashboard designed for speculative price forecasting or tax compliance. By consolidating fragmented passive income streams into a single, clear ledger, we isolate pure revenue without bloating interfaces with unnecessary asset tracking features that obscure visibility.

### Positioning
**The Dedicated Earnings Checkpoint**
Unlike generic investment platforms that blend yield data with market speculation and complex holding views, YieldScope acts as the undisputed first mover by defining a dedicated category for aggregated earnings verification:
- **Specialized Aggregation:** We focus exclusively on consolidating scattered rewards from specific chains and exchanges, respecting user intent without overwhelming them with irrelevant financial instruments.
- **Revenue Clarity Over Noise:** Our interface isolates pure yield income, deliberately stripping away bloated visualizations that hide small but frequent payments lost behind the noise of larger dashboard interfaces.
- **First-Mover Ownership:** By targeting a specific gap left by major portfolio tools, we establish clear ownership in the niche where other solutions fail to provide accurate reporting for CEX earn programs and staking networks simultaneously.

### Audience
- **Primary: Builders & Power Users** – Individuals actively earning liquidity incentives on platforms like Binance or OKX who also manage staked assets (e.g., Monad) across multiple protocols. They require precise, consolidated data to verify what they actually earned versus speculative holdings.
- **Secondary: Yield Optimizers** – Traders and DeFi participants looking for a simplified layer of truth regarding their passive income streams without needing full suite integration or complex tax logic upfront.

### Not for
We are explicitly not designed as a comprehensive portfolio management tool, nor do we serve users seeking speculative price forecasting, broad asset comparison platforms with generic performance metrics, or end-users requiring immediate tax filing suites within the application itself.

### Core Values
1. **Clarity Above All** – We believe in presenting only what matters: accurate earnings data. Complexities that obscure revenue should be removed to give the user a single source of truth.
2. **Reliability** – In an ecosystem fragmented by different exchange APIs and staking protocols, we stand as the trusted checkpoint where users can have confidence in their reported figures without second-guessing the math.
3. **Simplicity for Power Users** – Our philosophy is that sophisticated earning strategies deserve a simple reporting layer. We strip away unnecessary bloat to focus strictly on yield optimization and ledger accuracy.
4. **User Intent Respect** – We do not force users into irrelevant features like price charts or portfolio valuation tools when their immediate need is to track where money came from, ensuring our tool aligns perfectly with how builders manage scattered rewards.

This primary drive ensures that every piece of information presented is rigorously verified, stripping away the noise of speculative price forecasting to reveal only what actually matters: real earnings derived from active work on Binance/OKX or staking rewards on Monad/LUNC. Together, they create a persona that respects the user's time and intelligence while standing firmly against industry trends that overcomplicate yield tracking for speculative purposes.

- **Direct** — We strip away unnecessary financial jargon and dashboard bloat to focus strictly on the bottom line revenue; Example: A simple ledger view that shows pure yield, ignoring asset price volatility or irrelevant trading PnL.

- **Specialized** — We own the specific niche of fragmented reward aggregation with deep expertise rather than offering generic investment tools; Example: Explicitly distinguishing between exchange liquidity rewards and network staking yields without conflating them into a single metric.

## 5. Design principles

Prioritize clarity on actual revenue earned over complex asset tracking or speculative price forecasting, as our users need a trustworthy answer to "how much did I earn?" rather than a bloated dashboard of irrelevant data points. Design interfaces that strip away tax suite bloat and unnecessary charts to isolate pure yield streams, ensuring the first-mover position remains focused on accurate earnings reporting without overwhelming power users with noise.

### 2. Accent Discipline
Reserve the electric cyan accent #00efff strictly for critical actions or verified earnings confirmations to maintain a direct and trustworthy tone consistent with the Sage archetype. Do not use this high-contrast color for backgrounds, borders, or non-essential UI elements; instead, let it appear sparingly only when highlighting new rewards found in fragmented protocols or confirming a calculation result.

### 3. Tokens are the Contract
Treat every visual design decision as immutable code within DESIGN.md, mapping directly to CSS variables so that tokens remain the absolute authority over brand appearance across light and dark modes alike. Refrain from hardcoding colors like #00efff in HTML or JSX files; if a specific hex needs adjustment globally, update only the token definition to ensure structural integrity and prevent drift between environments.

### 4. Light and Dark First-Class
Engineer layouts where content density, contrast ratios, and accent usage feel equally natural and confident whether on a bright screen or a dark OLED display without needing separate theme overrides. Avoid design patterns that require specific background colors for text to be readable; instead, use semantic tokens that adapt intelligently so the "earnings checkpoint" experience feels seamless regardless of ambient lighting conditions.

### 5. Whitespace as Confidence
Utilize generous margins and breathing room around data rows to signal authority and precision, proving we are not afraid of empty space because our value proposition is robust enough to stand on its own. Do not clutter the interface with dense grids or fear-based compression techniques; allow the ledger lines for Binance rewards and staking yields their moment so users can digest numbers without cognitive overload.

### 6. Authenticity Over Generic Templates
Craft interfaces that respect the specific workflows of builders earning liquidity incentives, avoiding generic fintech clichés like hero banners, stock photo backgrounds, or overly playful gradients typical of consumer crypto apps. Stick to a stark, professional aesthetic that mirrors the reliability of an institutional ledger rather than adopting trendy templates designed for mass-market speculation, ensuring users feel seen by a brand built specifically for their niche needs.

### 7. Agent-Transmissible Brand
Structure all visual definitions as structured exports capable of being handed off cleanly from human designers to autonomous agents, enabling consistent regeneration of the ecosystem's checkpoint tools without style loss or interpretation drift. Design components that can be parsed and reassembled by code-based workflows, ensuring a new YieldScope instance in another protocol looks identical because its identity is defined by logic rather than manual asset manipulation.

## 6. Layout principles

- Section spacing — Use `xl` (40px) gaps between major content blocks to create a calm reading rhythm that prevents data fatigue; use `md` (16px) for component-level separation within cards or lists. This generous breathing room signals confidence and reduces cognitive load, allowing users to focus on the core metrics without distraction.
- Content max width — Constrain text-heavy sections between 720px–840px centering them for optimal readability; wider containers are reserved strictly for dashboard grids where comparative analysis is required, preventing horizontal scrolling that disrupts flow. This disciplined framing suggests an organized mindset and respects the user's attention span by presenting information in digestible chunks.
- Hero structure — Follow a vertical stack pattern: H1 headline (value proposition) → concise subcopy explaining the "earned" context → single primary CTA or dashboard preview; avoid multi-column headers that fragment focus immediately upon entry, ensuring the path to data is linear and frictionless for power users scanning quickly between sessions.
- Grouping / muted backgrounds — Utilize subtle `md` background tints (e.g., 5% off-white on light modes) only under distinct functional zones like "Recent Earnings" or "Stake Alerts," keeping the primary canvas crisp white; group related data points within single cards using consistent shadow elevation (`sm`) rather than border-heavy tables, fostering a clean visual hierarchy that feels modern yet utilitarian.
- CTA prominence — Primary actions (e.g., "View Yield Report") must utilize solid `primary` color with full radius on touch targets or sharp corners on desktop to indicate precision; secondary links remain text-only or outlined (`lg`) to prevent accidental clicks, reinforcing a brand personality that is direct and efficient rather than manipulative.
- Navigation patterns — Employ a sticky top bar for persistent access to "Earn" vs. "Stake" toggles with clear active states, avoiding deep nesting levels; since the audience consists of builders moving between chains (Binance/OKX/Monad), navigation should feel like a single-page app experience where depth is minimized in favor of immediate metric retrieval, reflecting transparency and trustworthiness.
- Footer / pricing — Reserve footers for essential legal disclaimers regarding cross-chain yield data accuracy or affiliate disclosures rather than extensive feature lists; use compact grid cards for optional premium tiers if applicable, ensuring they do not interrupt the vertical flow until the user has had a chance to explore their earnings potential naturally.

The overall layout strategy reinforces YieldScope's trustworthy personality by eliminating visual clutter and prioritizing clarity over decorative fluff. By enforcing strict max-widths and generous section spacing, we signal that this tool offers an honest, unvarnished view of what was earned without the noise of unnecessary metrics or salesy overlays. The directness is further expressed through high-contrast CTAs and a predictable navigation structure; when a power user knows exactly where to look for their yield data within two seconds of landing, it builds immediate confidence in the platform's reliability as a single source of truth across fragmented ecosystems.
