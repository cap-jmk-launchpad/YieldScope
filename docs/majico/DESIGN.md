## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
YieldScope expresses a trustworthy and direct personality with a Sage-led identity supported by Everyman.

Narrative anchor: We help Builders and power users who earn on Binance/OKX and stake on Monad (and LUNC), and want one trustworthy answer to what they earned — not a full portfolio or tax suite. with YieldScope — Solving scattered rewards. Positioning intent: No direct competitors were identified within this specific niche regarding scattered rewards aggregation from major exchanges and staking networks, so YieldScope establishes a first-mover position based on its unique brand profile.

YieldScope serves builders and power users who earn liquidity incentives on Binance or OKX while simultaneously staking assets like Monad and LUNC across decentralized protocols. These customers need only one trustworthy answer regarding their actual earnings rather than a complex portfolio dashboard designed for speculative price forecasting. The platform solves the problem of lost visibility by consolidating fragmented passive income streams into a single, clear ledger that isolates pure revenue without bloating interfaces with unnecessary asset tracking or tax suite features.

This tool stands out by focusing exclusively on yield optimization and accurate earnings reporting rather than generic investment performance metrics. It creates new space in the market by acting as a dedicated checkpoint for scattered rewards where other tools fail to see small but frequent payments lost behind interface noise. The brand claims ownership of this fragmented sector through specialized aggregation that respects user intent without overwhelming them with unwanted data visualization or irrelevant financial instruments.

YieldScope positions itself as the undisputed first mover in the ecosystem by defining and owning the category of dedicated earnings checkpoints for scattered crypto rewards.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Background (#ffffff)**: Base canvas and long-form reading areas.
- **Background Muted (#f4f4f5)**: Subtle section fills and low-emphasis surfaces.
- **Primary Text (#0a0a0a)**: Default text color for headings and body content.
- **Secondary Text (#52525b)**: Supportive labels, metadata, and helper copy.
- **Accent (#00efff)**: Primary actions, links, highlighted states, and key emphasis.
- **Accent On (#0a0a0a)**: Foreground text/icon color used on top of accent surfaces.
- **Accent Muted (#e2beff)**: Hover, pressed, and secondary accent interactions.
- **Accent 2 (#ffb3a1)**: Secondary harmony color for charts, badges, or decorative contrast.
- **Accent 3 (#c8e175)**: Tertiary harmony color for richer visual hierarchies.
- **Border (#e4e4e7)**: Strokes, dividers, and input outlines.

Dark-theme anchor colors include Background (#0a0a0a), Text (#fafafa), and Accent (#00efff).

## 3. Typography Rules
Headlines use **IBM Plex Sans** to create a recognizable top-level voice and strong hierarchy.
Body copy uses **IBM Plex Serif** for readability across long-form and UI microcopy.
Use heavier weight for section titles and medium/regular weights for supporting copy to preserve contrast.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** Primary buttons are accent-led (background #00efff, foreground #0a0a0a) with clearly readable contrast.
* **Cards/Containers:** Surfaces use #f4f4f5 with subtle border separation (#e4e4e7) to maintain structure without visual noise.
* **Inputs/Forms:** Inputs use quiet borders, clear focus states in accent tones, and high-legibility text treatment.

## 5. Layout Principles
Favor generous spacing between sections, keep core actions visually prominent, and maintain consistent alignment in multi-column and stacked layouts.
Use muted backgrounds for grouping and reserve accent intensity for actions, links, and high-priority information.
